About

PlotFinder is a website for managing and finding cemetery plots. The project uses MongoDB, a NoSQL document database, as its main data storage. The system was built for a real cemetery — Our Lady of the Way Eternal Garden in Magallanes, Cavite — as the client. The website has a cemetery map powered by Google Maps, a visual cemetery layout with section and plot grids, a reservation form, and pages for plot types, team info, and about the system. The goal is to make it easier for visitors to find and view plot availability without needing to visit in person. Admin management is handled directly through MongoDB Compass.
